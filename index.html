Who Are We?

The Heavies Track Club (HTC) is a Dublin-based training group specializing in middle- and long-distance running. Describing itself as “Ireland’s heaviest Mid/Long distance training group,” HTC is led by Simon Devenney, a Level 2 Endurance Running Coach and Senior Technical Officer at Dublin City University’s School of Health & Human Performance
